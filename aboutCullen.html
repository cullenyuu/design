Cullen's Story

I am Cullen

Graduated from University Tunku Abdul Rahman with a major in Finance and have 4 years experience in accounting.

In 2019, I have made a decision to change my career field. This is a big step—a leap of faith. The first struggle that comes to mind when considering a career change is leaving behind my years of experience and starting over. But –

“My fear of regret is stronger than my fear of anything else.It gives me courage.”

Discovered my Story

I have always had a love for creativity from a very young age - Love art, Love photography, Love creation.

I wish to start a new career which can infuse energy into my work life, and after a few weeks of “self-research”-Graphic Designer is my final choice.

To have a basic knowledge of design is a must before I leave the accounting field. Then I started searching for a course to learn basic art and design skills. After a couple months of searching,I finally found an Evening Part-time Design course from TAP (Training Academy for Professionals) and it is a corporate training entity owned by IACT College.

“This is where I start my design journey.”

Kick start my journey

I learned the essential graphic design software needed in the market, understand the principles and theory of Graphic Design. I also participate in online and professional graphic design communities, sharing artworks, have discussions and most importantly to increase my connections in the industry and knowledge of the field.

Starting something new, while scary, can be life-giving and motivating. It can be a great change of pace and can cause me to go from hating or tolerating my job, to loving work.

“Be Confident, Have Courage and jump out from the comfort zone !!”
